Description

Pure Black and White Rug for Indoor and Outdoor Extra Large 28" x 44"

This is the rug you need to feel at home as you cross your threshold into the entry of your house each and every day. Its large scale checkerboard print feels comforting and peaceful. And, it's equally as beautiful in contemporary homes as it is in vintage home decors.

It's the rug that will take you through every season of the year! In winter, it'll bring merry cheer when placed at the fireplace with red poinsettias on a nearby plant stand. In spring, use as a doormat to accent your colorful floral wreath! When the summer heat rolls in, toss it on your kitchen floor so you'll have a comfy spot to stand while you're slicing watermelon! And, in the fall, it'll be so pretty layered with baskets full of pumpkins!

The Finer Details:

  • Made of 45% Cotton, 45% polyester and 10% viscose so it's hardy enough for outdoor use, but looks chic and plush indoors, too!
  • High quality stitching at the edges so no fraying over time.
  • 28 x 44 inches - much larger than our competitors' rugs! Great for double patio doors or for layering a welcome mat!
  • Fade free fabric, so use outdoors or indoors in the sunlight!
  • PURE Black and White unlike competitors who use EggShell or Cream colors. This is Bleached White!
  • Includes a FREE laundry bag for machine washing - keeps your rug symmetrical, with no shrinking!
